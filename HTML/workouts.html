Bodyweight Circuit

This beginner-friendly circuit builds foundational strength using bodyweight exercises.

For-Abs (Six-Packs)

1. Crunches (3 sets of 10 reps)

2. Plank (Hold For 30-60 seconds,3-5 Reps)

3.Hanging legs Raises(3-sets,15 Reps)

For-biceps

1. Barbell Bicep Curls(3-4 sets of 8 reps)

2. DumbBell Hammer Curls(3 sets of 10 reps)

3. Preacher Curls(3-4 sets of 12 reps)

For-Shoulders

1. DumbBell shoulder press (3 sets Of 8 Reps)

2. Literal raises (3 sets Of 10-15 Reps)

3. Front Raises(3 sets Of 8-10 Reps)
